About RealistikOsu!

Discover what makes our server special

Welcome to RealistikOsu!

RealistikOsu is a custom server for the popular rhythm game osu! that extends upon the core online experience with countless fun features, quality-of-life improvements, and a thriving community.

Since our launch in early 2020, we've grown from a small passion project into a vibrant community of dedicated players. We're committed to providing a free, open-source alternative that enhances your osu! experience without compromising on quality or features.

Whether you're looking to compete on leaderboards, download beatmaps instantly, or just hang out with friends, RealistikOsu has something for everyone.

Advanced Features

Beatmap Rank Requests

Request that your favorite beatmaps be ranked! Our community-driven ranking system allows players to submit beatmaps for consideration. Help shape the ranked pool and discover new challenges.

Once a beatmap is ranked, it becomes available for competitive play and contributes to player rankings. It's your chance to influence what maps the community plays.

RealistikBot

Meet RealistikBot, our in-game bot that's always ready to play! Challenge the bot to matches, use it for practice, or just have fun. It's a great way to warm up or test your skills when other players aren't available.

The bot adapts to different difficulty levels and game modes, making it perfect for players of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, RealistikBot is always up for a match.

Core Features

Leaderboards

Compete with players from around the world on comprehensive leaderboards. Track your progress, see how you rank globally and locally, and watch your skills improve over time.

Global Leaderboards

Experience leaderboards that span across all game modes. See who's dominating in Standard, Taiko, Catch the Beat, and Mania, all in one unified ranking system.

Community & Social

Multiplayer
