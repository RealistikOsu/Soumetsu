Server Rules

Guidelines for a fair and enjoyable experience

Play Fair, Play Together

These rules exist to ensure everyone has a fair and enjoyable experience on RealistikOsu. By playing here, you agree to follow these guidelines.

General Rules

No Cheating Software

The use of any external modifications that alter the behaviour of the client (such as cheating software, custom clients) is strictly prohibited.

No Tablet Driver Exploits

Exploiting any tablet driver "features" (e.g., anti-chatter) is strictly prohibited and will result in a 3rd strike punishment.

No Multiaccounting

Creating alternate accounts is prohibited and will result in banning of the multiaccount and restriction of your main account.

No Impersonation

Impersonating other players will result in your name being changed.

No Account Sharing

Sharing accounts is prohibited and will result in the restriction of both parties involved.

No NSFW Content

Including any kind of NSFW content within your avatar or banner will result in an instant ban.

We reserve the right to apply and alter these rules as we see fit.

Liveplay Requirements

If the staff team believes you to be suspicious, you may be requested to produce a liveplay. A liveplay is a way of confirming your skill is legitimate and verifying the absence of cheats.

You MUST be logged into RealistikOsu
Start your PC on the recording/stream
Both hands and monitor must be visible
Play at least 3 maps (topplays or near your skill)
Entire GUI must be visible (key overlay, hit error bar, score)
Show Task Manager after opening osu! and at the end
Everything must be in a single recording (no edits)
You must use our patcher

Note: Failure to meet even a single of these requirements will result in your liveplay being automatically invalidated.

Punishment System

Cheating

Cheating uses a 3 strike system:

1 1 month restriction + wipe of cheated gamemodes
2 3 month restriction + full account wipe
3 Permanent but appealable ban

Multiaccounting

Every multiaccount is permanently banned. Main account:

1 15 day restriction
2 1-1.5 month restriction
3 Permanent but appealable restriction

In-game Toxicity

Toxic behavior results in silences:

In-game silence (and Discord mute if applicable)
Silence times increase exponentially
Starting duration: 1 hour

Questions?

If you have any questions about the rules or need to appeal a punishment, feel free to reach out to our staff team on Discord.
